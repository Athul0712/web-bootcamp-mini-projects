The Best Novels I have read so far

My Fav Novels of all-time.

  1. Shadow slave

    2. This was the novel that drew me into the world of online novels. It's so captivating that I even skipped sleeping on multiple occassions just so I could keep being immersed in the story. But the updation rate was a bit slow, hence I have put this on hold so I acn read it again when it's completed

    You can read it here if you like: Shadow Slave

  2. Charm of Soul Pets

    3. Another really cool online novel that captures the beauty of reliance and companionship between a man and his contracted battle pets in a Fanstasy World. Though it had such an abrupt and sad ending that I was depressed for days after reading it and it has many world-building elements that doesn't make sense, I still really enjoyed reading it.

    You can read it here if you like: The Charm Of Soul Pets

  3. Beast Taming Starting from Zero

    4. Another really good online novel that captures the beauty of companionship and growth between a tamer and his beasts. The story really explains each powerup and does not gloss over the details. The story is engaging and the world-building is impressive, making it a memorable read.

    You can read it here if you like: Beast Taming Starting From Zero

Looks like I really enjoy fantasy novels with beast taming genre.
